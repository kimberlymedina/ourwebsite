FAMILY
The Family Food Service is a new network of Glasgow-based partners committed to tackling Holiday Hunger. It will provide food to families during the school holidays and outwith normal school hours. The service is currently made up of four partners; Move On, Heart of Scotstoun, Community Central Hall and Annexe Communities, but we hope this network will continue to grow over the next few months. If you are interested in accessing the Family Food Service, or partnering with us, please get in touch.
